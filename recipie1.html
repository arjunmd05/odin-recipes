Biriyani

Description

  • Fragrant basmati rice cooked with mixed vegetables, spices, and herbs. A celebration dish that tastes even better the next day.
  • This is an easy veg biryani version you can make in a single pot. Great with raita or plain yogurt.

ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice (rinsed, soaked 20 min)
  • 1.5 cups water (adjust for rice)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans, potato)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp biryani masala (or garam masala)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (optional)
  • Whole spices: 1 bay leaf, 2 cardamom pods, 3 cloves, small cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander + mint (small handful, chopped)
  • Fried onions (optional garnish)

Steps

  1. Rinse and soak rice for 20 minutes; drain.
  2. Heat oil/ghee in a pot; add whole spices and let them crackle.
  3. Add sliced onions; cook until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste; cook 30 seconds.
  5. Add tomato; cook until soft.
  6. Add mixed veggies, turmeric, chili powder, biryani/garam masala, and salt; stir 1â€“2 minutes.
  7. Add rice and water; gently mix. Taste liquid for salt.
  8. Bring to boil, then lower heat, cover, and cook until rice is done and water absorbed.
  9. Rest 5 minutes. Fluff gently.
  10. Top with coriander, mint, fried onions. Serve hot.
