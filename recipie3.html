Masala Dosa

Description

  • Thin, crispy South Indian crepes made from fermented rice and urad dal batter, filled with a spiced potato stuffing.
  • Served hot with coconut chutney and sambar—perfect for breakfast or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dosa rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • For potato filling:
    • 2-3 boiled potatoes
    • 1 onion, sliced
    • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
    • 1 tsp mustard seeds
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric
    • Few curry leaves
    • Salt to taste
    • Chopped coriander (optional)

Steps

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 4–6 hours. Grind into a smooth batter, add salt, and ferment overnight.
  2. For filling: Heat oil, crackle mustard seeds, add onions, green chilies, curry leaves, turmeric, and mashed potatoes. Mix well. Add salt and coriander.
  3. Heat a non-stick or cast iron tawa. Pour a ladle of batter and spread into a thin circle.
  4. Drizzle oil/ghee around edges. Cook until golden and crisp.
  5. Place potato filling in center. Fold dosa and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
