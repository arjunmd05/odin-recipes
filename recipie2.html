Sandwich

Description

  • Quick, tasty, and customizable — a sandwich is perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a snack.
  • This version is loaded with fresh veggies and simple seasoning, great for school or office lunchboxes.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white, brown, or multigrain)
  • 1 small cucumber, sliced
  • 1 small tomato, sliced
  • 1 boiled potato, sliced
  • Few onion rings (optional)
  • Butter or mayonnaise (1–2 tsp)
  • Green chutney (optional, 1 tsp)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cheese slice (optional)

Steps

  1. Spread butter or mayo on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Optional: spread green chutney on top of butter for extra flavor.
  3. Layer sliced veggies: cucumber, tomato, potato, onion.
  4. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper over the veggies.
  5. Add a cheese slice if using.
  6. Cover with the second slice, buttered side down.
  7. Grill or toast if desired, or serve as-is.
  8. Cut diagonally and serve with ketchup or chutney.
